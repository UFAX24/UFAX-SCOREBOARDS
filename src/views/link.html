What is a backlink?

What is a backlink? Backlink is the extension of a web site to different web sites in a tument, tune or any other way. Backlinks means getting links from a site to your site. Suppose, you have a web site and you put the link of that site on another site. Then you will get a backlink for your site from the site where you gave the link to your site. If your site is A and the site you link to is B, then you get a backlink from B site. In this way, the more links you give to your site, the more backlinks you will get. And it is considered as the most important thing in search engine optimization. In other words, we can say in one word that the incoming link we get from other sites to our own site is called backlink. Why do backlinks?
